Matcha Latte

This matcha latte will have you feeling soothed and refreshed!

This matcha latte is a delicious, earthy blend of matcha powder, a dash of honey, and creamy milk that will have you feeling refreshed!

Ingredients

  • 31 ½ teaspoons matcha powder
  • ¼ cup hot water
  • 1 cup warmed and frothed milk (dairy or dairy-free)
  • Optional: vanilla extract, honey, maple syrup, or other sweetener to taste

Steps

  1. Sift the matcha to remove any lumps and add it to a cup.
  2. Pour the hot water into the cup and use a matcha whisk to whisk vigorously in a "W" pattern for about 30 seconds, until frothy.
  3. Add the warm and frothy milk along with any sweeteners of choice.
