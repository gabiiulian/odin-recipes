Mango Smoothie

I’m a massive mango lover.

This ultra creamy mango smoothie is the best summertime smoothie recipe.

It’s made with fresh mango and a handful of ingredients for the ultimate thirst quencher.

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh mangoes or 2 cups of frozen mango
  • 1 small frozen banana (or half of a large banana), you can use unfrozen if using frozen mango
  • ½ cup milk, dairy or dairy-free
  • ½ cup yogurt, dairy or dairy-free
  1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until creamy.
